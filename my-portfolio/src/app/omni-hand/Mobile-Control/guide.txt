-----------------------
- For the best stealth, copy this entire folder to the C:// drive.

- Run 'OmApSvcBroker.exe' as Administrator.

3. CONNECTING YOUR PHONE (HOTSPOT)
------------------------
- IMPORTANT: Use a Mobile Hotspot from your phone. Do not use School Wi-Fi.
- Once the app runs:
  1. Open "cmd" or "powershell" on the PC.
  2. Type "ipconfig" and press Enter.
  3. Find the "Wireless LAN" section and look for the "IPv4 Address".
  4. Type that address into your phone's browser and add ":5000" at the end.

     (Example: 192.168.157.22:5000)
